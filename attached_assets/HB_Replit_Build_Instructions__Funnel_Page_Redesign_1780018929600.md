# Replit Build Instructions: `/home-builders` Funnel Page Redesign

**Project:** Graylock Digital Landing Page Optimization
**Target URL:** `graylockdigital.com/home-builders`
**Goal:** Increase conversion rate by shifting messaging from "features" to "business outcomes," improving social proof, addressing the DIY objection, and reducing form friction.

## General Design & Structure Guidelines

1.  **Mobile-First:** Ensure all sections, especially the hero and the lead form, are perfectly optimized for mobile devices. Buttons must be easily tappable, and text must be legible without zooming.
2.  **Clean & Simple:** Maintain a clean, modern aesthetic with ample whitespace. Avoid clutter that distracts from the primary Call to Action (CTA).
3.  **Consistent CTAs:** All primary CTA buttons should use the exact same color and styling to create a clear visual path for the user.
