Here is the full strategy. The core principle is simple: change what signals the audience, not what signals the value. Your pricing and homepage are converting well for your core market — the goal is to stop accidentally excluding other buyers without watering down what's working.
Part 1: Pricing Plan Names — The Fix
The current names (Solo Practice, Group Practice, Enterprise) describe a type of business, not a size of business. A roofing company owner, a law firm, or a consulting agency reads "Solo Practice" and immediately thinks "this isn't for me." The fix is to shift to names that describe scale, not specialty.
